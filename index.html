About Me

A warm welcome to my website!

Long time ago, nobody knew about today, and now we are fully blind about the upcoming future. But, in fact, there are scientists who steer human prediction of the world toward their deductions. Every nascent science has come up with a simplification and unification of many complicated phenomena that have a shared characteristic upon deeper inspection. I believe there is a single comprehensive goal that is widespread and shared in nature. While all of the attempts were in the direction of describing the whole world by a single equation to predict it in time, science did nothing but improving humanity's perception of the reality, very very slightly. I believe that the world system evolves in the most optimum way for the objective of “improvement”, regardless of its initial position, and will continue to move “forward” to its infinitely faraway goal under the direction of this eternal function.

My name is Faezeh Habibi, I am interested in learning to deepen my view into the dynamically changing path of the world to provide a unified way that crystallizes every predictable and surprising phenomenon; I believe there must be a link between every existing science that beautifully describes the world and perfectly disentangles twisted humanity’s knowledge of life.

It's my great honor to be a Neural Adaptive Computing (NAC) laboratory member.
Advised by Prof. Alex Ororbia
