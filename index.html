About Me

Hello everyone, welcome to my website!

Long time ago, nobody knew about today, and now we are fully blind about the upcoming future. But, in fact, there are scientists who axis the human prediction of the world towards their deductions. Every nascent science has come up with a simplification and unification of many complicated phenomena that have a shared characteristic upon deeper inspection. While all of the attempts were in the direction of describing the whole world by a single equation to predict it in time, science could do nothing but just improve the human perception very, very slightly. I believe that the world system evolved in the most optimum way for the objective of "demand for improvement", regardless of its initial position, which will remain in the same direction and infinitely moves “forward” to its goal.

My name is Faezeh Habibi, I am interested in learning to deepen my view into the dynamically changing path of the world to provide a unified way that crystallizes every predictable and surprising phenomenon; I believe there must be a link between every existing science that beautifully describes the world and perfectly disentangles twisted human knowledge of life.
